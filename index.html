My Favourite Meal

Name of the Meal:[Isbindi]

Ingredients

  • Liver
  • Onions
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Cooking Oil

Recipe

  1. Step 1:Chop the onions and the liver separatly.
  2. Step 2:Add the chopped onions and cooking oil and allowed to fry.
  3. Step 3:Add the chopped liver then let fry for 15mins or until the red colour has disappeared.
  4. Step 4:Afterwards add the salt and pepper then cook for 8 mins.
  5. Step 5:Its ready! dish up and enjoy.
